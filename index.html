About Our Town

Nestled in the scenic countryside northwest of Auckland, Kumeu is a vibrant rural township known for its rolling vineyards, fresh produce, and tight-knit community spirit. With a population that blends farming families, artisans, and newcomers seeking a relaxed lifestyle, Kumeu continues to grow while keeping its country charm intact.

The area is surrounded by fertile farmland, small orchards, and award-winning wineries. On weekends, the township comes alive with markets, horse shows, car rallies, and music in the park.
